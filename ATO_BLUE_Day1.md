## Situation

Gudauta starts **neutral with no garrison**. First side to land 4+ troops inside the zone captures it uncontested. A garrison AI group only spawns *after* a zone changes hands — so if Blue arrives first, the Chinook lands and drops troops with no clearing required.

Red's only realistic threat to Gudauta in the opening window is a helicopter from FH66 (33 km away). Blue's FH08 is 28 km from Gudauta — a 1–2 minute lead.

## Phase 1 — Gudauta Capture (H+0:00 to H+0:20)

### Heli Package

**H+0:00 — HEAVY 11 loads at FH08 (F10 → CTLD):**

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| 1× Infantry Group (4 troops) | §50 |
| 1× Shilka crate | §180 |
| **Total** | **§230** |

VIPER 11 departs immediately toward Gudauta while HEAVY 11 completes loading (~2 min).

**H+0:02 — HEAVY 11 departs.** Direct route to Gudauta, following VIPER 11's path.

**H+0:09 — Approach:**
VIPER 11 pushes slightly ahead on the final leg to sweep the zone perimeter.
- Zone is clear (most likely): VIPER 11 calls "CLEAR TO LAND."
- Red troops spotted: VIPER 11 engages. HEAVY 11 holds 2 km short until zone is clear.

**H+0:10 — Landing and capture:**
1. HEAVY 11 lands at the **edge of the Gudauta zone circle**.
2. Unload the Infantry Group — 4 troops deploy immediately and begin capture.
3. Drop the Shilka crate at the zone edge.
4. Build the Shilka crate (30 seconds). Shilka spawns as active defender.
5. Zone turns Blue once 4+ troops are inside with no Red units present.

**H+0:15 — HEAVY 11 departs and despawns.**
The Chinook lifts off, lands again inside the blue zone, and the player despawns. Phase 1 Chinook work is done.

**VIPER 11 stays.**
Hold orbit over Gudauta. Watch the southern approach (toward FH66, ~33 km). The Shilka handles low-slow threats; the Apache engages anything the Shilka cannot. While the zone is blue Viper 11 can rearm/refuel.

> **Note:** Gudauta has no FARP in this ATO. VIPER 11 cannot rearm at Gudauta — manage ammo carefully. If Winchester, return to FH08, but this leaves Gudauta uncovered.

## Contingencies

**Red beats Blue to Gudauta (Red garrison spawns):**
- A Red AI garrison spawns inside the zone when Red captures it.
- VIPER 11 must destroy the Red garrison before HEAVY 11 can insert troops.
- Call BLADE for a CAS pass if the garrison is hardened or VIPER 11 is low on ammo.
- Accept the delay — contest the zone, then retake it.

**VIPER 11 is lost before Phase 3 SA-8 arrives:**
- Gudauta is held only by the Shilka and 4 troops.
- BLADE provides CAS coverage from the air.
- Priority: accelerate HEAVY 11 Phase 3 sortie to get SA-8 on site.

**BLADE is intercepted en route:**
- JSOWs are standoff — launch from max range and egress before committing to the CAP box.
- If BLADE cannot reach FH66: Red helicopter threat to Gudauta remains. VIPER 11 must remain alert.
